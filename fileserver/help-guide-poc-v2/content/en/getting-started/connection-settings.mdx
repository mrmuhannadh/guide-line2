## Reconnect

If you are disconnected from the system, you may reconnect using the following steps.

**STEPS**

1. Click on the **Connection** icon on the bottom left corner of the window.
2. Once the **Connection** is clicked an overlay is open
3. Click on the option **Reconnect** under **Connection** section

## Log Out

If you want to Log out from the system, you may Log out using the following function.

**STEPS**

1. Click on the **Connection** icon on the bottom left corner of the window.
2. Once the **Connection** is clicked an overlay is open
3. Click on the option **Log out** under **Connection** section

4. When you click the option **Log out** it prompts the Log out confirmation dialog box with the following options as given below:

   - **Save & Log out**
   - **Log out without Saving**
   - **Cancel**

   #### Save & Log out

   When you click **Save & Log out**, it prompts a Save Workspace dialog box to save the workspace as given below with the following options:

   1. Save as Default
   2. Save
   3. Cancel

5. When you click either **Save As Default** or **Save** the workspace will be successfully saved and Log out from the system
6. When you click **Cancel**, the workspace will not be saved and **Log out** from the system

   #### Log out without saving

7. When you click **Log out without saving**, the workspace will not be saved and **Log out** from the system

   #### Cancel

8. When you click **Cancel**, will not be **Logged out** from the system

## Exit

There are two options to manipulate **Exit** in the system, they are **Exit** directly from the dashboard which is located on the top right side below the Market Summary Panel and the **Exit** via the **Connection** section.

If you want to Exit from the system, you may Exit using the following function.

### Option 1

**STEPS**

1. Click on the **Connection** icon on the bottom left corner of the window.
2. Once the **Connection** is clicked an overlay is open
3. Click on the option **Exit** under **Connection** section

4. When you click the option Exit it prompts the Log out confirmation dialog box with the following options:

   - **Save & Exit**
   - **Exit without Saving**
   - **Cancel**

   #### Save & Exit

   When you click **Save & Exit**, it prompts a Save Workspace dialog box to save the workspace with the following options:

   1. Save as Default
   2. Save
   3. Cancel

   #### Exit without saving

   1. When you click **Exit without saving**, the workspace will not be saved and **Exit** from the system

   #### Cancel

   2. When you click **Cancel**, will not be **Exited** from the system

### Option 2

**STEPS**

1. Click on the icon at the top right corner of the window, below the _Market Summary_ panel
2. Click on the option **Exit** from the menu items
3. An _Exit Confirmation_ window will be prompted with the following options:

   a. **Save & Exit** – Save the current workspace and exit from the application

   b. **Exit without Saving** – Exit from the application without saving the workspace

   c. **Cancel** – Cancel the exit process
