# Get Screen Shot

<FigureProvider>
Take a screen shot of the current state of the workspace and make annotations, if required, using this function.

### Take a Screen Shot

STEPS:

1. Click on the camera icon at the top right corner of the window.
2. This opens the screen shot editor window where you can make annotations.
3. Click on **Save** to save the image to a preferred location of your choice.

<Figure
  baseName='screenshot/screenshot'
  alt='Screen Shot'
  description='Screen Shot'
/>

### Make Annotations to Screen Shot

STEPS:

1. In the screen shot editor, under **Add Texts**, enter any text you wish, specify the **Font**, **Font Size**, **Text Color**, and click **Enter Text**.
2. This will add the text on to the image. If you wish to add a new text, repeat from the step 1.
3. To remove the previously added text, click **Undo Text**.

<Figure
  baseName='screenshot/add-text'
  alt='Screen Shot Add Texts'
  description='Screen Shot Add Texts'
/>

4. To draw a shape on the image, under **Draw Shapes**, specify the **Line Size**, **Border Color** and if it is a **Rectangle**, **Ellipse** or **Trend Line**, and draw on the image.
5. If you wish to draw a new shape, repeat from step 4.
6. To remove the previously drawn shape, click **Undo Drawing**.
7. Click on **Save** to save the image to a preferred location of your choice.

<Figure
  baseName='screenshot/add-shape'
  alt='Screen Shot Draw Shapes'
  description='Screen Shot Draw Shapes'
/>
