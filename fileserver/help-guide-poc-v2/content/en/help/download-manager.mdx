### Download & Export Details

STEPS:

1. Tick on the records you wish to download.
2. Click on the Download button at the bottom of the window or click on the download icon in front of the record.
3. Once the record is downloaded successfully, the icon will change.
4. Click on the icon to export record as an Excel document.

### Filter Details

STEPS:

- **Filter by Data Intervals** – Filter trade details by a selected data interval: 1min intraday, 15min intraday or history 1 day.
- **Filter by History Time Period** – Filter trade details to select multiple files by a selected history time period up to: None, 6 weeks, 3 months, 6 months.
