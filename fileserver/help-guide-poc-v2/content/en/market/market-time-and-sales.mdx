## Filter details

- Click on the icon to open the filter criteria to be filled, select to filter by **Price**, **Quantity**, **Turnover**, **Time**, enter the **From** **To** values and **Mark Above**, **Mark Below** values, and click **Apply** as in <FigureRef label='Filters' />.
- Tick on the **Mark Successive Trades** checkbox to mark successive trades.
- Tick on the **Filter Successive Trades** checkbox, to display only successive trades.
