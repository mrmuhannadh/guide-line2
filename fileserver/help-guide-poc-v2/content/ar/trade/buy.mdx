## Buy an Order

> [!NOTE]
>
> - The Buy function of an order can be invoked in several windows.
> - Wherever there is a <font color="#449552">**green**</font> color button in any window, this would invoke the Buy Window.

STEPS

1. Invoke the Buy Window.
2. Search/enter the symbol you wish to buy from, under the **Symbol** field.
3. Select the **Portfolio**.
4. Select/enter all other required fields to buy the order (order type, quantity, price, TIF, etc.) and click on the **Buy** button at the bottom of the window as in <FigureRef label='Buy Window' />.
5. Once clicked on the **Buy** button, a confirmation window will let you click on **Confirm Order** to confirm the buy as in <FigureRef label='Confirm Order' />.
6. Once confirmed, the order will be listed under the Order List.
7. **Price Information** of the selected symbol will be listed on the right side of the window.
8. Click the **Reset** button to reset all values to its default values.
9. Click the **Clear** button to clear all entered values.
10. Add the order to a Basket by clicking on the basket button at the bottom left corner of the window as given below,
