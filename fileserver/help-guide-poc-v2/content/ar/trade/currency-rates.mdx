## Filter Currency Rates & Decimal Places

STEPS

1. Click on the **Currencies** drop down to list down all available currencies.
2. Select all required currencies to be listed and compared with and click **Save**.
3. Select the number of decimal places to be displayed in the charts by entering the value under the **Decimal Places** field.
